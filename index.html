About.

My name is Josh and I've always had a passion for building things and watching things grow. I enjoy the process of drawing out plans and seeing them come to life. I find there are a lot of similarities between the preparation, execution and problem solving of gardening/wood working, to those of Web Development.

Gardening

I've always enjoyed making things grow. What interests me most is the different needs for each plant and how they react to certain variables. You can tell what a plant needs by understanding what it's structure or it's leaves are telling you.

Technology

I've always had an interest in technology, from wanting to build my own PC, to finding interest in coding and wanting to build my own website. I love to find out how things work and to use that knowledge to build my own.

Wood Works
